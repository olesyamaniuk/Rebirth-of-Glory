Rebirth of Glory is an immersive survival strategy game where you must rebuild civilization after a devastating invasion.

Rebirth of Glory is an immersive survival strategy game where you must rebuild civilization after a devastating invasion.

  • As a leader, you will guide your people through a land filled with supernatural forces, deadly creatures, and unforgiving terrain.
  • Construct shelters, gather resources, and develop a stronghold to protect your people from external and internal threats. Make critical decisions, form alliances, and uncover the secrets of the holy village while fighting to reclaim your people's honor.
    Will you rise from the ashes and forge a new era of glory?
  • Your survival story begins now!

Key Features

  • Settlement Management

    Construct and expand a thriving village in an unpredictable world.

  • Survival Elements

    Gather food, manage resources, and ensure the safety of your people.

  • Tactical Combat

    Defend your village against vicious beasts and supernatural threats.

  • Weather and Environment

    Adapt to extreme weather conditions that can either help or hinder your progress.

  • Story-Driven Gameplay

    Uncover the mysteries of the invasion and the sacred land you now call home.

  • Job System

    Assign villagers different roles such as hunters, craftsmen, warriors, and healers.

Main Characters

  • Elder Mira

    A wise advisor who provides knowledge about the sacred village and its hidden dangers.

    A skilled fighter who leads the village’s defense against wild beasts and raiders.

  • The Hostile Tribe Leader

    The main antagonist who sees your people as trespassers and threatens to wipe out your settlement.

  • Lena, the Healer

    A compassionate caretaker who helps keep villagers healthy and treats the wounded.
